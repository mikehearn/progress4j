ProgressTrackerResetter

class ProgressTrackerResetter<T : ProgressReport.Tracker>(factory: Callable<T>) : ProgressReport.Tracker, AutoCloseable

A callback that delegates a stream of reports to another callback that it creates, and re-creates when a completion is delivered.

This allows you to just send a continuous stream of reports with completion mixed into the middle, whilst maintaining the invariant that a progress tracker won't receive events after an operation is considered to be completed.
